About Me

Hello! I am Gavin Corkery, I was born in Commerce, MI and now I live in Trenton, MI. I got into programming when I was a freshman in high school where I taught myself HTML and CSS and fell in love with coding and developing different things. I didn't focus on many other languages until my senior year of high school when I took AP Computer Science which was focused around making small video games in JavaScript, from that class I knew for sure that I wanted to be a software engineer and develop programs or websites that helped people and served a purpose for them. After high school I attended Henry Ford College in Dearborn, MI where I studied for my associates in Computer Science. In college I took classes on JavaScript, C, C#, Python, HTML, and CSS.

With 5 general study classes standing in my way of graduation, I chose to take a break from school and further my programming knowledge with a Full-Stack boot camp at devCodeCamp.

Transaction Tracker This application allows a user to have ledgers which is intended to represent a bank account or a credit card. Users will then be able to add transactions to their ledgers and assign them a category. Once a category is created, a user can view transactions on that specific ledger that fall under that category. Users are encouraged to set goals on their categories which allows them to see how they are fitting a goal for their category. JavaScript
Trash Collector This project is intended to be a new hub for trash collection companies. This application provides users with a beautiful front-end to interact with which makes their time spent on the page more comfortable, gone are the days of basic HTML pages from multi-billion dollar industries. Along with improvements for the consumer, there is a new portal for employees. On their homepage, they will see a google map view of all of their stops for the day, and only that day. This new interface allows an overhead view of where they will be going on their route, and allow them to "complete" a customer pickup which will remove their pin from the map. JavaScript
Car eCommerce This application is a reinvention of sites like CarGurus and other online car resellers. A customer is able to purchase a car from either our company fleet, or users can purchase cars that are for sale by other users. With integration from the PayPal API, customers are able to have a seamless, fast, and secure checkout. JavaScript
